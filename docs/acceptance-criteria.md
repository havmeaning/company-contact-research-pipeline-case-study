# Acceptance Criteria

## V2.1 regression criteria

- No harmful false-positive record is published in the controlled sample.
- No harmful high-confidence error is published.
- No intermediary or portal contact is represented as a direct company contact.
- Every published contact field remains bound to its evaluated source.
- Candidate contact fields are not mixed across unverified domains.
- Header handling preserves the first company record.
- Deterministic replay and regression checks complete successfully.
- Integrity verification identifies the expected controlled artifacts.
- Unsupported cases produce an explicit unresolved outcome.
- Public-safe packaging excludes confidential and reconstructive content.

## Documentation criteria

- Every major results section names the risk-based sample limitation.
- No claim extends results to all approximately 499 records.
- No production, runtime-improvement, financial, adoption, endorsement, or independent-validation claim is made.
- No executable source code or private dataset is included.
- Metrics match the supplied baseline and V2.1 evidence exactly.
- Original diagrams are accessible, self-contained, and free of confidential identifiers.

## Status

The supplied V2.1 evidence records 17/17 replay and regression tests passing on the controlled, risk-based sample. This is acceptance of an intermediate validation release, not production acceptance.
